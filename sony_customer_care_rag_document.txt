Sony Customer Care Knowledge Base (India)
Version date: 2026-07-10
Purpose: RAG source document for a Sony customer-care support assistant.
Scope: Sony India electronics support for consumer questions about TVs, home theatre,
headphones, audio devices, cameras, camcorders, accessories, storage media,
PlayStation support routing, product registration, warranty, service booking,
repair status, and escalation preparation.


Important assistant behavior:
- Give calm, step-by-step support.
- Ask for the product model, serial number or IMEI, purchase date, invoice or
  warranty reference, and a short issue description before advising repair.
- Do not promise warranty approval, replacement, refund, or free repair.
- For current pricing, warranty eligibility, and live appointment slots, send the
  customer to official Sony support or the service center locator.
- For urgent electrical safety problems, tell the customer to unplug the product
  and avoid using it until inspected by authorized service.
- If the customer is outside India, explain that this document is India-focused
  and route them to the Sony support site for their country.

Section: Quick Routing
Intent phrases: contact sony, customer care number, call support, email sony,
live chat, talk to agent, help desk
Sony India customer support can be reached through official self-help pages,
live chat, email/contact form, phone support, service booking, service center
locator, and repair status tracking. For quick self-help, route customers to
Sony India's support page and ask them to search by product model.

Section: Contact Channels
Intent phrases: phone number, helpline, live chat hours, support timings,
customer care timing, IVR options
Sony India lists the customer-care phone number as 080 6500 6500. The contact
page lists IVR Option 1 for demo and installation and IVR Option 2 for other
queries. The phone support availability shown on the contact page is 9:00 AM to
7:30 PM. Live chat support is available for questions about TV, camera, audio
device, or accessory categories, with hours shown as 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. If chat
agents are occupied or chat is offline, ask the customer to refresh later or use
phone/email support.

Section: Product Categories
Intent phrases: product type, supported product, television, camera, headphones,
audio, car, marine, computer, mobile, accessory, storage, playstation
The Sony India support page organizes support by product type. Common categories
include televisions and projectors, cameras and camcorders, headphones/audio and
video, car and marine entertainment, personal computers, mobile phones and
tablets, PlayStation, accessories, storage and recording media, business and
professional products, and other products. For professional broadcast or business
products, route the customer to Sony professional product support.

Section: Self Help Before Service
Intent phrases: fix online, troubleshoot, manual, firmware, software update,
FAQ, questions and answers, support app
Before arranging repair, ask the customer to try official self-help resources:
search for the model on Sony support, check the manual, review Q&A articles,
run troubleshooting guides, and install firmware or software updates when
available. Recommend the Support by Sony mobile app for product information and
update notifications. Do not tell the customer to install firmware unless the
model page confirms that an update applies to their exact model.

Section: Warranty Overview
Intent phrases: warranty, standard warranty, extended warranty, overseas
warranty, warranty terms, warranty claim, warranty card
Sony products may be covered by Sony's standard manufacturer warranty. Warranty
terms depend on product type, country, purchase channel, date of purchase, and
valid documentation. Ask the customer for invoice, warranty card, product model,
serial number, purchase date, and seller information. If warranty eligibility is
unclear, advise the customer to contact Sony support or an authorized service
center. For extended or overseas warranty questions, route to Sony India's repair
and warranty section.

Section: Out Of Warranty Repairs
Intent phrases: out of warranty, paid repair, repair cost, service charges,
expired warranty, paid service
For products outside warranty, Sony India says customers can contact Sony to
arrange repairs through Sony authorized service centers. Service charges may
apply. Ask the customer to check official service charges and confirm costs with
the service center before approving a repair. Do not estimate repair cost without
current official pricing.

Section: Service Appointment
Intent phrases: book service, service booking, appointment, home service,
installation, demo installation, wall mount, repair visit
Sony India's book service page supports online appointment requests. It lists
home demo-installation for LCD TV and home theatre systems, and home service for
television and home theatre systems. Appointment forms may ask for appointment
type, product model, serial number or IMEI, purchase date, warranty reference,
whether the product is under warranty, dealer details, preferred appointment
date, issue comments, name, email, phone, address, postal code, state, and city.
The page says Sony will get back within the next 48 hours for applicable service
requests.

Section: Track Repair Status
Intent phrases: repair status, track repair, job number, service job sheet,
repair job number, installation status
To track repair or installation status, the customer needs the 8-digit Repair
Job number from the Service Job Sheet and the contact number used during repair
registration. The tracking page may show fields such as service type, serial
number, model name, email address, repair job number, job type, product model,
serial number, and description. If the customer does not have the job number,
ask them to check the service sheet, SMS/email from Sony, or contact support.

Section: Service Center Locator
Intent phrases: service center near me, authorized service center, repair
location, locator, nearest sony service
Use Sony's service center locator for the nearest authorized service location.
Ask for product category, city, state, and PIN code if helping the customer find
the correct route. Remind the customer to confirm opening hours, appointment
requirements, and accepted product categories before visiting.

Section: Product Registration
Intent phrases: register product, my sony, track warranty, product registration,
register camera, register audio, extended warranty registration
Sony India product registration is available for select product categories.
Registration can help customers manage Sony products, track warranty-related
information, receive firmware updates, and access applicable offers. The
customer may need to create an account, sign in, enter product details, and
upload or provide invoice details. Registration offers and warranty extensions
can have eligibility windows and product-model restrictions, so always ask the
customer to verify the exact terms on the registration portal.

Section: Common TV Questions
Intent phrases: bravia, tv not turning on, no picture, no sound, wifi not
working, app not working, remote not working, hdmi issue, installation
For BRAVIA or Sony TV issues, ask for model name, screen size, purchase date,
software version if available, connection type, and whether the issue affects
all inputs or only one app/device. Basic safe steps: restart the TV, check power
and HDMI cables, test another input/source, check network connection, replace or
test remote batteries, and check the model support page for firmware updates.
For wall-mounting, installation, repeated power failure, burning smell, damaged
screen, liquid exposure, or internal hardware issues, route to authorized
service.

Section: Common Headphone And Audio Questions
Intent phrases: headphones pairing, bluetooth not connecting, no sound,
charging issue, left earbud not working, noise cancelling, speaker not charging
For headphones, earbuds, soundbars, wireless speakers, or audio systems, ask for
model name, connected device, app used, firmware version if known, and whether
the issue happens with multiple phones/computers. Basic safe steps: charge the
device, reset or initialize using model-specific instructions, remove and pair
Bluetooth again, test with another device, check the Sony app, and look for
firmware updates on the official model page. For battery swelling, overheating,
water damage, or physical damage, stop use and contact service.

Section: Common Camera Questions
Intent phrases: alpha camera, lens issue, camera not turning on, memory card,
focus problem, firmware update, sensor, warranty camera
For Sony cameras and camcorders, ask for body model, lens model, battery model,
memory card type, firmware version if known, error message, and whether the
problem appears with another battery, lens, or card. Basic safe steps: charge or
swap battery, reseat lens and memory card, check camera settings, reset settings
if appropriate, and check official firmware for the exact model. Do not suggest
cleaning the image sensor unless the customer is comfortable with the official
instructions; otherwise route to service.

Section: Warranty Exclusions And Cautions
Intent phrases: warranty void, serial number removed, water damage, accidental
damage, unauthorized repair, normal wear, data loss, third party accessories
Warranty and extended warranty terms can exclude issues such as unauthorized
modifications, altered or removed serial numbers, external accessories or
cosmetic parts, improper installation, incompatible third-party equipment,
third-party software or recorded media issues, normal wear and tear, accidents,
lightning, water ingress, fire, acts of God, excessive shock, poor ventilation,
and other external causes. Sony India warranty terms also advise customers to
back up data because service may affect stored content and Sony is not
responsible for loss of data or business opportunity. Apply these points as
general caution, not as a final eligibility decision.

Section: Escalation Preparation
Intent phrases: complaint, escalation, unresolved, bad service, repair delayed,
case pending, replacement request
For escalation, collect customer name, registered phone and email, product model,
serial number, purchase date, invoice copy, warranty card if available, repair
job number, service center name, dates of previous contact, photos or videos of
the issue, and a short timeline. Summarize the customer's request clearly:
repair, replacement review, refund review, status update, installation, or
technical guidance. Route the customer to Sony's contact form or customer-care
phone line and advise them to keep the case or repair job number for follow-up.

Section: Answer Style Examples
Intent phrases: example answer, response format, support tone
Example for warranty: "I can help you check the next step. Please share the
model number, serial number, purchase date, and whether you have the invoice or
warranty card. Warranty approval must be confirmed by Sony support or an
authorized service center."
Example for repair status: "To track the repair, use the 8-digit Repair Job
number from your Service Job Sheet and the phone number used during registration.
If you do not have the job number, check Sony's SMS/email confirmation or call
080 6500 6500."
Example for unsafe power issue: "Please unplug the product and avoid using it.
Because this may involve electrical safety or internal hardware, contact Sony
authorized service before trying further troubleshooting."
